Welcome to Golden Tex

Golden Tex is the 100% export oriented Buying House, manufacturer & Exporter with extensive experience in Bangladesh. The company has started its successful operation from 1991. It has elaborated it’s compasses from little to large by gradually. It has the team of highly motivated anddedicated professional Technical Persons and QCs to provide our buyer with the highest quality products.

We ensure all activities of sweater & Knit Garments (T-Shirts), Woven manufacturing are done through documented quality management system at every stage. Golden Tex also follow the standard operating procedures in every process to meet buyer’s requirement properly.
